bayard

Questionnaire builder

I worked closely with a special projects team at Bayard (known as KROW, "work backwards") to design from scratch a completely new and revolutionary way to create questionnaires that could manage complex logic, tagging, and data capture all while maintaining a feeling of simplicity and flexibility.

These questionnaires are used to pre-screen job applicants for companies that tend to hire year-round and non-stop. The main goals of these tools was to eliminate the often hated "upload your resume, now enter all of the same information in again..." scenarios that online job applicants so often run into.

This tool was required to be used internally by the KROW team and their account managers, so power user functionality was a must... but overall still needed to be accessible by new recruiting account leads for their key customers like Petco, Chili's, Sysco, and Carmax. We utilized a flexible, infinite canvas, with drag-and-drop node creation functionality that allowed users to make questionnaires with as simple or as complex logic as designed. These questionnaires could then be tracked for performance, interview scheduling, and candidate communication.
