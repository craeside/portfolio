Viasat

Design system - "BEAM"

Most large teams find that a design system - while necessary for aligning dispersed teams and products - is a large effort. At Viasat, I was a part of the the team that took on the initiative (from the ground up), and worked to design and document our core components and atomic elements that would create the foundation for our design system, BEAM.

This design system was formed to solve a problem that had began to compound and grow over recent years. As Viasat continued to develop more and more custom software to enable the business to grow, it's individual products began to diverge. Initially more of a hardware-focused company, Viasat worked in a variety of areas and started to see the need for a more unified end-user experience.
