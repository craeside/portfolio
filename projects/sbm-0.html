design web and native products for workforce productivity management

Unfortunately, this is another one of those top secret projects.

While I can't get into the details, or show you everything I created during my time at SBM, I can talk a bit about how ambitious this project has been.

Creating a better work environment for soft services employees is a big task. They are often performing their duties off hours, and even when they are working “normal” hours they are often ignored. How then do you recognize and encourage your best employees?

In improving the methods by which these people are graded, we can bring to the front the employees who work hard day in and day out. We can also open the door to larger data views, and beneficial insights at the corporate level.

Who:
SBM Management Services

What:
A responsive web application and a native mobile app

When:
Q3 of 2015 - Q1 of 2017

Where:
Sacramento, CA

Why:
Establish data collection and measurement mechanisms to improve the pay scale of service industry workers

& How:
Photoshop/Sketch, Angular Bootstrap library, Invision, Framer

So what exactly were we making?

Here’s an overview of some cool stuff I made, without giving away any trade secrets:

  • a clock in, clock out Android app
  • financial review tools for budgeting hours and supply spends
  • a reward system for paying out bonuses to entry-level staff
  • so many dashboards
  • training and onboarding workflows
  • complex navigation studies
  • on site, in person interviews with custodial and managerial staff
