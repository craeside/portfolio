Windows VPN widget

Working with the Secure Network Operations team at Viasat, I completely overhauled the visual design of a duel-tunnel secure VPN widget for Windows-based Toughbooks. The initial look and feel of the widget was missing crucial affordances, and was jarring in the context of its target users.

The app went through several iterations, and the initial "we don't want to change too much" attitude coming from the product ownership team slowly turned to excitement over the end visual result that could be possible with this app. Ultimately we were able to ship a much more modern app, with dark and light modes that were OS-dependant, all in less than two months.

In addition to the full redesign of the widget, I also did all of the frontend code for the widget inside of a Python app called QT Designer (the main developer's preferred program to create the Windows application). Thankfully, the program allowed for ~mostly~ normal css2-grade styling and I was able to make sure the designs were pixel-perfect myself.
