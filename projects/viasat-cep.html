Sales Enablement Tools

Working with the Customer Enablement Platform (CEP) team at Viasat, I worked in an integrated role within the development team to help improve the usability, visual design, and functionality of our agent-facing sales tools in Salesforce. I worked with the team through 2020-2021 creating and improving components throughout the existing salesforce processes.

Sales tools have always been near and dear to my heart, since I worked in sales and retail all through college before starting my career in design. I've tried to bring that experience to the table when crafting components that make agent's jobs easier and more efficient. In addition to directly designing simple and fast workflows, I also look for areas to advocate for transparency and care to be added to these end-user's work lives.
