Viasat

Encryption management

I worked with the Secure Network Operations team at Viasat, helping to improve an existing encryption device management tool. As more complex features were needed, I helped improve the Material design-based tool with better visualizations and more accessible workflows.

This project mostly focused on improving the feedback methods of interactions within the application, as well as a simplification of the workflows by improving UX copywriting and by providing "wizards" where needed for more complex actions.

Ultimately, clear data visualizations and human-readable alerts and feedback made an impact for users who for years had been dealing with an antiquated and cumbersome tool. The new design created a bridge between complicated hardware with back-breaking manuals and instructions and everyday humans that just needed their equipment to work. A clear understanding of the underlying rules and functionality allowed our team to provide simplified processes devoid of jargon and unnecessary information.
